About LunchBox

History of LunchBox

The team at LunchBox were all freshmen at Drexel University studying Computer Science and Information Systems. They all had a common goal and came together to create this web application. As inexperienced college freshmen, the team wanted to be able to cook for themselves since dining hall food is not great and have a space to share ingredients that they have made themselves in their dorms. Join other young adults by using LunchBox, the web app that helps you create a healthy balanced meal.
